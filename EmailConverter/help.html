What is this program?
It is a python e-mail reader that allows you to convert your messages into .csv file (.txt but .csv structure) using imaplib and PyQt5 library.

How to start?
1.Write your login, password and your e-mail IMAP server and click "login". This operation might take some seconds.
2.Then you can choose a folder and fields that you want to download and just click download.
3.When progress bar will reach 100%, your data will be saved in "saved.txt".

IMAP Server
The most common question is "What is this and how do I find it?"
Shortly - it is an internet protocol that can access your mail box and then send or retrieve messages.
To find it open your email's "Options" or "Settings" and then look for "IMAP," "forwarding" or "mail servers." For example, to enable IMAP in Gmail, go to "Settings," select the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab, scroll down to the IMAP Access section and select "Enable IMAP."
You might also have to allow unauthorised application to access your e-mail.

Fields
These are containers in which parts of your e-mail message data are stored. Just pick the fields you need, you don't have to select them all. The most basic ones are already chosen as default (From, To, Content etc.).

My folder is not showing up
It may happen when your folder have an illegal folder name (characters such as (*,;,{,} etc.)) you can try to change your folder name in browser and try again.
